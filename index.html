about ||| Gregg Andrews

For as long as I can remember, art has been a part of my life. At an early age, I would watch Bob Ross on public television and try to replicate his paintings with my watercolors. It was not long after that endeavor, I picked up a pair of drum sticks, which lead me further on my journey of self-expression. I’ve been blessed over the past twenty-five years, to have lead various creative-art programs. During that time, I gained experience in media and graphic design. My life outside of work focuses on my family of six. I am married to a wonderful woman who is an English teacher for an online high school. Almost daily, the topic of our conversations is User Interface and Design. We are blessed with four daughters who are self-professed "nerds" that love all things tech and express themselves through various forms of art. In 2006, we moved to Austin. We immedialty feel in love with the beauty of the Hill Country and the vibrant music scene. We live on Willie’s golf course and embrace the laid-back Spicewood lifestyle. I am an artist at heart who strives to communicate truth with excellence in all that I do. My hope is that through my talents and service, I can make this world a better place. Thank you for taking the time to check out my website. I would love the opportunity to bring your web design ideas to life!

ideas

I collaborate with clients and peers to nurture and transform ideas into well thought out design specs. After all, that's where the majority of amazing user experiences start.

user design

I sketch and wireframe interfaces focusing on content structure, intuitive user designs and simple interactions. My design asthetic bends toward minimalism. I truly believe that sometimes, less is more.

coding

I design in the browser with HTML, CSS and a touch of JavaScript. I love coding things from scratch, but I can work with front-end frameworks like Bootstrap too.
