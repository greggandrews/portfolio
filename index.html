about ||| Gregg Andrews

For as long as I can remember, art has been a part of my life. At an early age, I would watch Bob Ross on public television and try to replicate his paintings with my watercolors. It was not long after that endeavor that I picked up a pair of drum sticks, which lead me further on my journey of self-expression. I’ve been blessed over the past twenty-five years to have lead various creative-art programs. During that time, I gained experience in media and graphic design. My life outside of work focuses on my family of six. I am married to a wonderful woman who is an English teacher for an online high school. Almost daily, the topic of our conversations is User Interface and Design. We are blessed with four daughters who are self-professed "nerds" that love all things tech and express themselves through various forms of art. In 2006, we moved to Austin. We immedialty fell in love with the beauty of the Hill Country and the vibrant music scene. We live on Willie’s golf course and embrace the laid-back Spicewood lifestyle. I am an artist at heart who strives to communicate truth with excellence in all that I do. My hope is that, through my talents and service, I can make this world a better place. Thank you for taking the time to check out my website. I would love the opportunity to bring your web design ideas to life!

ideas

I collaborate with clients and peers to nurture and transform ideas into well thought out design specs. After all, that's where the majority of amazing user experiences start.

user design

I sketch and wireframe interfaces focusing on content structure, intuitive user designs and simple interactions. My design asthetic bends toward minimalism. I truly believe that sometimes less is more.

coding

I design in the browser with HTML, CSS and a touch of JavaScript. I love coding things from scratch, but I can work with front-end frameworks like Bootstrap too.

process ||| we partner together to find solutions

step 1 - analysis & planning

Understanding the needs of our client is the first step we take, but also one of the most important aspects in any collaboration! We will analyze the options together and we will suggest solutions that advantage you every time. Together we will grow and develop harmoniously, capturing clients for your business, which will generate you a satisfying income and cover investment. Through analysis and research we will succeed in always giving you good solutions that will get you earnings and through which you can cover each investment you did with the help of our professional team. By doing a detailed analysis and planning together, we will reach the best results for your business.

step 2 - design & development

An attractive and optimized website for all platforms and the modalities to access it can represent a difference all your clients are looking for in a real online jungle. In the online jungle we know today, the majority of websites are optimized and tend to have an attractive design, so they can satisfy the needs of the clients and to attract as many customers as possible. So the image is very important. Your website represents the business card you show to your clients. That’s why our priorities are creating an easy to use and accessible website for your clients, and also creating a state of flux that increases exponentially the possibility of the client to return on the website.

step 3 - launch & grow

The last step is launching your website into the world. Now it’s time to start building buzz for when you’re ready to unveil it to the world. Before we publish your website, we will check it for accuracy to assure that it fits with your brand’s style. Then, we will test it out by drafting marketing messages and sending them out to a select audience. Whether we create your website from scratch or redesign your existing website, we will optimize the value of your product and message for your target audience. Once your website is up and running, don’t forget to celebrate. A successful website launch is the culmination of planning and a lot of hard work. Reward yourself and your team for a job well done!
